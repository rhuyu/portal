About Me

Hello! My name is Randy Huynh and I am a current emerging professional in the Year Up Program at Seattle Central College. I am currently learning front-end languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and plan on later learning how to create and automate test cases for Quality Assurance. I take pride in receiving a professional of the week certification early on in the program through my skills in community building and helping others. I enjoy group-based assignments and activities which make me feel confident to be a part of any team! Please feel free to reach out on LinkedIn and connect!

The Year Up program allows for young adults such as myself to be able to go from working minimum wage jobs, with little to no room for growth, to a full blown career all within a year! The program does this through a strategy of putting us emerging professionals through six months of learning and development followed by another 6 months of an internship that is assigned to us. Effiecntly, accelerating us into a meaningful career within one year!
